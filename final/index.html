About Me
Hey there, I'm Nikita. I'm a senior double majoring in cognitive science and data science. Reading has always been a huge part of my life! I particularly love young adult contemporary, adult literary fiction, magical realism, and any and all things queer. Please enjoy this silly little website where I talk about my 2022 reading year, including my top books and the reading challenges I participated in. Hope you find some recommendations!
